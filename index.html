Welcome to the Monta Vista
Game Development Club!

Meetings every Wednesday lunch at E203

The Monta Vista Game Development Club is a great opportunity for video game and programming enthusiasts alike to learn about the inner workings of video games!

Throughout the year, we'll get to learn about what makes the gears in your favorite games turn, what the process of game development looks like, and how to make them yourself.
No prior experience needed!

As a team, you'll get to compete in international Game Jams for a chance to win various prizes.


Come stop by for a fun time!
